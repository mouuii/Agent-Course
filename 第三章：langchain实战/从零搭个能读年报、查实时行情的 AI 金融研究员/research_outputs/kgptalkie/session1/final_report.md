# Amazon Q1 2024 Revenue Summary

## Overview
Amazon.com, Inc. reported its financial results for the first quarter of 2024, which ended on March 31, 2024. The company demonstrated strong revenue growth across its major business segments [1].

## Revenue Analysis
Amazon's total consolidated net sales for Q1 2024 reached **$143,313 million**, representing a significant performance for the start of the fiscal year [1].

The revenue is broken down into the following primary segments:
*   **North America:** $86,341 million
*   **International:** $31,935 million
*   **AWS (Amazon Web Services):** $25,037 million

## Key Takeaways
The AWS segment continues to be a critical driver of revenue, contributing over $25 billion in the first quarter alone. The North America segment remains the largest contributor to the company's overall net sales [1].
