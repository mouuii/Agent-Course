## Segment-level analysis: Performance of AWS, North America, and International segments (2023-2024)

### Focused Query 1: Amazon segment performance 2023 AWS North America International revenue operating income
In 2023, Amazon saw a significant recovery in profitability across its retail segments and continued growth in AWS.

*   **North America Segment:**
    *   **Net Sales:** $352.8 billion, a 12% increase compared to 2022 ($315.9 billion).
    *   **Operating Income:** $14.9 billion, a massive turnaround from a $2.8 billion operating loss in 2022.
    *   **Drivers:** Increased unit sales (particularly from third-party sellers), advertising growth, and subscription services, partially offset by technology and infrastructure investments.

*   **International Segment:**
    *   **Net Sales:** $131.2 billion, an 11% increase (11% excluding FX) from $118.0 billion in 2022.
    *   **Operating Loss:** Improved significantly to a $(2.7) billion loss, compared to a $(7.7) billion loss in 2022.
    *   **Drivers:** Growth in unit sales and advertising, similar to North America, though still impacted by fulfillment and shipping costs in international markets.

*   **AWS Segment:**
    *   **Net Sales:** $90.8 billion, a 13% increase from $80.1 billion in 2022.
    *   **Operating Income:** $24.6 billion, up from $22.8 billion in 2022.
    *   **Drivers:** Increased customer usage, although growth was tempered by enterprise cost optimization efforts and long-term contract pricing changes.

### Focused Query 2: Amazon 2024 segment performance AWS North America International (Q1-Q3)
2024 has been a year of accelerating growth for AWS and sustained profitability for the retail segments.

*   **AWS Segment (Acceleration):**
    *   AWS revenue growth accelerated throughout 2024, reaching **19% YoY** in Q3 2024 ($27.5 billion).
    *   **Operating Income:** AWS operating income reached $10.4 billion in Q3 alone. For the first nine months of 2024, AWS generated $29.2 billion in operating income, already surpassing its full-year 2023 total.
    *   **Efficiency:** Profitability was boosted by a change in the estimated useful lives of servers (reducing depreciation expense) and decreased payroll-related expenses.

*   **North America Segment:**
    *   Continued steady growth: $86.3B (Q1), $90.0B (Q2), and $95.5B (Q3).
    *   **Operating Income:** Consistently profitable, generating $15.7 billion in the first nine months of 2024, compared to $8.4 billion in the same period of 2023.

*   **International Segment (Profitability Pivot):**
    *   The International segment turned a corner into profitability in 2024.
    *   **Operating Income:** $2.5 billion for the first nine months of 2024, compared to a $(2.2) billion loss in the same period of 2023.
    *   Q3 2024 operating income was $1.3 billion.

### Summary
Amazon's segment performance in 2023 and 2024 highlights a shift from post-pandemic recovery to high-efficiency growth. The **North America** and **International** segments moved from operating losses in 2022 to substantial profitability by 2024, driven by advertising and fulfillment efficiencies. **AWS** remains the primary profit engine, with growth accelerating to 19% in late 2024 as enterprises ramped up cloud and AI spending, supported by improved cost structures including extended server lifespans.
