### Sources for Theme 4: Key Growth Drivers and Strategic Investments (2023-2024)

**1. SEC Filings (Historical Data)**
- **Amazon 10-K (Full Year 2024):** 
  - Revenue breakdown: Advertising ($56.2B), AWS ($107.6B), Third-party services ($156.1B).
  - CapEx: $77.7 billion in 2024 vs. $48.1 billion in 2023.
  - Anthropic investment details: $1.25B (Q3 23), $2.75B (Q1 24), $1.3B (Q4 24).
- **Amazon 10-Q (Q3 2024):**
  - Advertising revenue for nine months ended Sept 30, 2024: $38.9B.
  - Discussion on fulfillment efficiencies and lower transportation costs.
- **Amazon 10-Q (Q2 2024):**
  - Segment growth rates: AWS (19%), North America (9%), International (7%).
  - Mention of change in estimated useful life of servers (impacting depreciation).
- **Amazon 10-Q (Q1 2024):**
  - Initial 2024 AI investment commentary and Bedrock scaling.

**2. Key Metrics & Quotes**
- **Advertising Revenue Trend:** 
  - 2022: $37,739M
  - 2023: $46,906M
  - 2024: $56,214M
- **Fulfillment Efficiency:** "The increase in fulfillment costs... is primarily due to increased sales... partially offset by fulfillment network efficiencies." (Q3 2024 10-Q).
- **AI Commitment:** Total commitment to Anthropic reaching over $5B by the end of 2025.
- **CapEx Driver:** "We expect cash capital expenditures to increase in 2025, primarily driven by investments in technology infrastructure." (10-K 2024).

**3. Strategic Context**
- **Logistics:** Shift to "regionalization" to reduce shipping distances and costs.
- **AI Infrastructure:** Focus on custom silicon (Trainium/Inferentia) to reduce dependency on third-party GPU providers and lower costs for AWS customers.
- **Project Kuiper:** High R&D spend noted in Technology and Infrastructure for satellite broadband.
