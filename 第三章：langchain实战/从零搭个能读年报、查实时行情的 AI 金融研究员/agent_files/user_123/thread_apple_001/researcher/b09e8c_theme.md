## [Theme 5] Apple's Capital Return Program and Cash Position (2023-2024)

### Focused Query 1: Apple capital return program share buybacks and dividends 2023 2024
Apple maintained a massive capital return program throughout fiscal years 2023 and 2024, consistently returning value to shareholders through both share repurchases and cash dividends.

- **Fiscal Year 2024 Performance:**
  - **Share Repurchases:** Apple repurchased 499 million shares of its common stock for a total of **$95.0 billion** during the year.
  - **Dividends:** The company paid **$15.2 billion** in dividends and dividend equivalents. 
  - **Major Announcement:** On May 2, 2024, the Board authorized an additional program to repurchase up to **$110 billion** of common stock, the largest in the company's history.
  - **Dividend Increase:** Also on May 2, 2024, the quarterly cash dividend was raised by 4% from $0.24 to **$0.25 per share**, continuing the trend of annual increases.
- **Fiscal Year 2023 Performance:**
  - **Share Repurchases:** Apple spent **$77.6 billion** on repurchases of common stock.
  - **Dividends:** The company paid **$15.0 billion** in dividends and dividend equivalents.
  - **Dividend Rate:** The quarterly dividend was $0.24 per share for most of the year (raised from $0.23 in May 2023).

### Focused Query 2: Apple total cash and marketable securities balance sheet 2023 2024
Apple maintains a strong liquidity position, though it continues to work toward its stated goal of reaching a "net cash neutral" position over time.

- **Fiscal Year 2024 (as of Sept 28, 2024):**
  - **Cash and Cash Equivalents:** $29.9 billion.
  - **Current Marketable Securities:** $35.2 billion.
  - **Non-Current Marketable Securities:** $91.5 billion.
  - **Total Cash & Marketable Securities:** **$156.7 billion**.
  - **Total Debt:** $106.6 billion (consisting of $10.0B commercial paper, $10.9B current term debt, and $85.8B non-current term debt).
  - **Net Cash Position:** ~$50.0 billion.
- **Fiscal Year 2023 (as of Sept 30, 2023):**
  - **Cash and Cash Equivalents:** $30.0 billion.
  - **Current Marketable Securities:** $31.6 billion.
  - **Non-Current Marketable Securities:** $100.5 billion.
  - **Total Cash & Marketable Securities:** **$162.1 billion**.
  - **Total Debt:** $111.1 billion.
  - **Net Cash Position:** ~$51.0 billion.

### Focused Query 3: Apple operating cash flow and free cash flow 2023 2024
Apple's ability to generate cash from operations remains one of its most significant financial strengths, providing the fuel for its capital return program and investments.

- **Operating Cash Flow:**
  - **2024:** Generated **$118.3 billion** in cash from operating activities (an increase from 2023).
  - **2023:** Generated **$110.5 billion** in cash from operating activities.
- **Capital Expenditures (Capex):**
  - **2024:** Payments for acquisition of property, plant and equipment were **$9.4 billion**.
  - **2023:** Capex was **$11.0 billion**.
- **Free Cash Flow (Operating Cash Flow - Capex):**
  - **2024 Free Cash Flow:** **$108.9 billion**.
  - **2023 Free Cash Flow:** **$99.5 billion**.

### Summary
Apple's financial strategy in 2023 and 2024 was characterized by record-level cash generation and aggressive capital returns. Despite a slight dip in net income in 2024 compared to 2023 ($93.7B vs $97.0B), operating cash flow increased to $118.3 billion. This robust cash flow allowed Apple to return over $110 billion to shareholders in FY 2024 alone ($95B in buybacks and $15.2B in dividends). The company's total cash and marketable securities stood at $156.7 billion at the end of FY 2024, with a net cash position of approximately $50 billion, maintaining a slight reduction from the prior year as it moves closer to its long-term net-cash-neutral target.
