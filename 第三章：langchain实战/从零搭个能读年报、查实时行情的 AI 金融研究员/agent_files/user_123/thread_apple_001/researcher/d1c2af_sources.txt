### Sources for Apple Revenue and Net Income Trends (2023-2024)

**1. Apple Inc. 10-K (Fiscal Year Ended September 30, 2023)**
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR (Source file: apple 10-k 2023.md)
- **Key Data:**
    - Total Net Sales: $383,285 million
    - Net Income: $96,995 million
    - Diluted EPS: $6.13
    - Gross Margin: $169,148 million
    - Notes: Fiscal year 2023 had 53 weeks; 2022 had 52 weeks.

**2. Apple Inc. 10-K (Fiscal Year Ended September 28, 2024)**
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR (Source file: apple 10-k 2024.md)
- **Key Data:**
    - Total Net Sales: $391,035 million
    - Net Income: $93,736 million
    - Operating Income: $123,216 million
    - Gross Margin: $180,683 million
    - One-time Tax Charge: $10.2 billion related to the State Aid Decision (Ireland tax case) announced Sept 10, 2024.
    - Effective Tax Rate: 24.1% (compared to 14.7% in 2023).

**3. Apple Inc. 10-Q for Q1 2024 (Three Months Ended Dec 30, 2023)**
- **Note:** Extracted from comparison tables in subsequent 10-Qs and 10-K.
- **Key Data:** Net Sales: $119,575M, Net Income: $33,916M.

**4. Apple Inc. 10-Q for Q2 2024 (Three Months Ended March 30, 2024)**
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR (Source file: apple 10-q q1 2024.md - *Note: Search results incorrectly labeled this as Q1, but it contains Q2 March 30 data*)
- **Key Data:**
    - Total Net Sales: $90,753 million
    - Net Income: $23,636 million
    - iPhone sales: $45,963 million (-10% YoY for the quarter).

**5. Apple Inc. 10-Q for Q3 2024 (Three Months Ended June 29, 2024)**
- **URL:** SEC EDGAR (Source file: apple 10-q q2 2024.md - *Note: Search results incorrectly labeled this as Q2, but it contains Q3 June 29 data*)
- **Key Data:**
    - Total Net Sales: $85,777 million
    - Net Income: $21,448 million
    - Services Revenue: $24,213 million.

**6. Analysis of Ireland State Aid Case (Found in 10-K 2024)**
- **Details:** On September 10, 2024, the European Court of Justice (ECJ) set aside a previous judgment and confirmed the European Commission's 2016 State Aid Decision. Apple recorded a one-time income tax charge of $10.2 billion (net) in Q4 2024. This represented $15.8 billion payable to Ireland, partially offset by a $4.8 billion U.S. foreign tax credit.
